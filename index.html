Welcome to the dfohub Brand Contest!

You need a Web3 Enabler to access this site!

For this contest, our community will come together and decide on a new logo, one that will better express the culture and mission of DFOhub. Before contributing, we recommend carefully reading the DFOhub Explainer Website paying particular attention to the Manifesto Statement.

This contest can be followed both here and on Cryptovoxels! All of the NFT entries will remain in the parcel gallery perpetually, memorializing the cool artists who participated in this contest forever! PS: You can find some easter eggs there!

Structure

The contest has two phases:

Phase 1: Quadratic Funding Artist Competition

Begins 6pm CEST 06/27/2020 and ends ~3am CEST 08/05/2020 (~38 days) at the Ethereum Block n 10600000

Phase 2: $BUIDL Holders and Core Team Final Decision

Begins 6pm CEST 08/07/2020 and ends 3am CEST 08/22/2020 (15 days)

Phase 1 - Quadratic Funding Artist Competition

The first phase of the competition is designed to incentivize Artists to create as much artwork as possible for the DFOhub logo.

To vote for an artwork, users can use the brandcontest.dfohub.com GUI to donate small amounts of ETH directly to the artist.

Note: Donations made outside of the brandcontest.dfohub.com GUI (without using the donation buttons there) are not counted.

At the end of Phase 1, the top 3 artists will receive the following rewards:

#1st 🏆 1 $ETH, 300 $BUIDL, and 200 $ARTE

#2nd 🏆 0.5 $ETH, 100 $BUIDL, and 60 $ARTE

#3rd 🏆 0.2 $ETH, 50 $BUIDL, and 30 $ARTE

Everyone can participate by creating a Non-Fungible Token (NFT) using the deployment section of ethart.org. The cheaper, regular type of NFT and the more expensive, onchain type are both allowed.

How to create an NFT using ethart:

#1 In the ‘Title’ box, name your design.

#2 In the ‘Description’ box, tell us how your design reflects DFOhub’s culture.

#3 Upload your design as the ‘Cover’ image (png) and choose a background.

#4 Choose between minting your NFT as Regular or Onchain.

#5 Choose between minting your NFT as ARTE or ETRA.

#6 Upload an explainer PDF, or a Vectorial File or PSD, AI File as the ‘NFT File’.

#7 Deploy!

Note: Every NFT sent without a Description, a Cover image and a PDF / Original Vectorial File will be sent to the 0x0000000000000000000000000000000000000000 address and will be not entered into the contest The same is true of any NFT sent that does not have a correlation with the DFOhub Brand Challenge.

After creating your NFT, the next step is to send it to this wallet: 0x74Ef70357ef21BaD2b45795679F2727C48d501ED

In case you’re not familiar with NFTs, you can easily send it by doing the following:

#1 Go to ethart.org.

#2 Click the OpenSea link next to your NFT (listed under ‘Your NFTs’).

#3 On the OpenSea page, click GIFT and transfer your NFT to the wallet.

And that’s it!

Sent successfully, your NFT is automatically entered into the contest, and users can donate ETH to vote for your design.

Phase 2 - $BUIDL Holders and Core Team Final Decision

BUIDL holders and the Core Team will be able to vote for the winning brand using their $BUIDL. Note: Phase 2 doesn't have any impact on the Phase 1 rewards.

TL;DR

The current logo is the ghost emoji with a rainbow background for two reasons:

#1 Anonymous Applications. DFOhub resolves unaddressed questions about security in the dApp ecosystem. This allows for startups with anonymous founders. Basically, people can create and manage dApps without needing to know or trust each other. This is why we chose the Ghost emoji.

#2 Censorship-Resistance. DFOhub is developing a base layer for dApps to be independent from each issuer. For this new wave of dApps, independence is not merely a hope, but an assured basic security layer. We chose the rainbow because it is a clear image that expresses diversity; no one can be censored or blocked from the application.

These two images will now change, but their spirit will live on. We can't wait to see a better version of the DFOhub Logo!

This is your chance to propose a new way to express DFOhub’s principles.
